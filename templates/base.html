How DeepFake Detector Works

Our Detection Process

1

Upload Your Media

Select either Image or Video mode and upload your file. We support common formats including JPG, PNG for images and MP4, MOV, AVI for videos.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced neural networks analyze the media for manipulation markers, inconsistencies, and digital artifacts that indicate deepfake creation.

3

Instant Results

Receive a clear verdict on whether the media is authentic or manipulated, along with a confidence score showing the certainty of our analysis.

4

Ethical Assessment

For detected deepfakes, we provide an Ethical Score that evaluates the potential harm and manipulation severity on a scale from 0-100.
